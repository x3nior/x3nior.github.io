Welcome to the Gamepasses!

In Blox Fruits, gamepasses are special purchases that enhance gameplay by offering unique abilities, boosts, or other advantages. These can include features like increased experience gain, extra storage for items, or access to exclusive fruits and areas. Players can buy these gamepasses using Robux, the in-game currency of Roblox. They can significantly improve your experience and help you progress faster in the game!

2x Boss Drop Chance
 350
This game pass doubles the player's chance for a Boss NPC to drop an item such as Swords, Accessories,...

For example, if the drop chance of an item was 10%, it would be increased to 20%. However, the initial drop rate up to now has not yet been verified, just know that this game pass will increase the drop rate to x2.

Fast Boats
 350
This game pass gives the player access to two exclusive boats, Miracle and The Sentinel.
Miracle
  • Free to spawn.
  • Small boat.
  • Second fastest Boat in the game.
  • Two seats.
 The Sentinel
  • Costs 1,000 to spawn.
  • Large boat.
  • Fastest boat in the game.
  • Five seats.
2x Money
 450
As the name suggests, this game pass doubles the amount of Money the player makes in-game.

However, this only applies to NPCs and Quests, not to chests found around the map.

2x Mastery
 450
This game pass doubles all mastery EXP received when defeating NPCs. This only affects the amount of EXP gained towards item mastery, not levels.

For example, if defeating an NPC normally gives 20,000 EXP, with this game pass, the player will instead receive 40,000 EXP towards their mastery.

Dark Blade
 1,200
Purchasing this game pass will give the player a Dark Blade.

This sword is also upgradable to its second, better version, by completing The Son Quest.

Fruit Notifier
 2,700
This game pass allows the player to locate Blox Fruits when they spawn into the server the player's is currently in. It does not give the player a map, but rather tells them (in meters) how far they currently are from the fruit that spawned.
