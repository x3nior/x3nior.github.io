WELCOME TO METRIC CONVERSION

The metric system is a system of measurement that is a decimal system. The current international standard for the metric system is the International System of Units (Système international d'unités or SI) and Metric Conversion refers to the conversion of the given units to desired units for any quantity to be measured. The different unit of measurement resulted in the introduction of the "Metric System". The metric units followed in the United States are called United States Customary Units (USCS).
